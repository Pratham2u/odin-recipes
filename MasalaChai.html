Masala Chai

Description

Masala chai (literally "mixed-spice tea") is a popular, aromatic Indian beverage made by brewing black tea with a blend of aromatic spices like cardamom, ginger, cinnamon, cloves, and black pepper, combined with milk, water, and a sweetener (usually sugar). Its flavor profile varies widely as spice proportions differ by household and region, but the core is a strong, flavorful, spicy, and milky tea often served hot.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 ½ teaspoons sugar
  • 1 whole cardamom pod
  • 1 whole clove
  • 2 black peppercorns
  • 3 teaspoons black tea leaves
  • ½ cup warm milk

Steps

  1. Combine the water and sugar in a small saucepan, and bring to a boil.
  2. Add the cardamom pod, clove, peppercorns and tea leaves.
  3. Remove from heat, and let the mixture steep for 2 to 3 minutes.
  4. Strain into a cup, and fill cup the rest of the way with milk.
  5. Sit back, relax, and enjoy!
