Oreo Milkshake

Description

An Oreo milkshake is a popular, indulgent, and creamy beverage made by blending Oreo cookies, vanilla ice cream, and milk, often resulting in a "cookies and cream" texture. It is frequently served as a treat, especially during warm weather, and is a staple in many cafes and fast-food restaurants.

Ingredients

  • 6 chocolate sandwich cookies(Oreo preferred)
  • 3 scoops vanilla ice cream
  • 3/4 cup milk

Steps

  1. Blend chocolate sandwich cookies, ice cream, and milk together in a blender until smooth. Pour into 2 glasses.
  2. Serve chilled with Oreo crumbs as garnish on top
